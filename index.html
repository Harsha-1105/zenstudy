Feeling Panic?

Trigger instant calm-down sequence

Emergency Calm Down (5-4-3-2-1 Grounding)

Take a deep breath. Let's redirect your focus and bring your awareness back to the present moment to break the panic cycle.

5

Things you can SEE

Look around and identify 5 distinct objects near you. Focus on their shapes, colors, or textures.

4

Things you can TOUCH

Feel 4 things around you. Your clothes, the desk, the chair, or the cool air on your face.

3

Things you can HEAR

Listen for 3 distinct sounds. The hum of a fan, birds chirping outside, or traffic in the distance.

2

Things you can SMELL

Notice 2 things you can smell. The scent of paper, coffee, pencil lead, or fresh air.

1

Thing you can TASTE

Focus on 1 thing you can taste. Take a sip of water, or focus on the current taste in your mouth.
